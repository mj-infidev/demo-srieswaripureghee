About Us

We invite you to become a part of the Sri Eswari Pure Ghee family. Taste the difference, feel the nourishment, and celebrate a legacy of wellness that has been cherished for generations.

Our Story: Tradition Reimagined

Long before ghee became a buzzword in modern health circles, it was already a timeless staple in Indian kitchens — treasured not just as an ingredient, but as a symbol of wellness, purity, and abundance. At Sri Eswari Pure Ghee, our journey began with a simple desire: to bring back the authentic, soulful ghee we grew up with - the kind our grandmothers made with care, patience, and love.

As life became faster and food more commercialized, we noticed something was missing — real ghee. The kind that's slow-cooked, rich in aroma, golden in color, and full of nourishing fats. So, we went back to the roots. We partnered with local farmers, embraced the traditional Bilona method, and started making small batches of ghee using pure A2 milk from indigenous cows. No shortcuts. No compromise.

Our Mission: Purity, Quality, Trust

At Sri Eswari Pure Ghee, our mission is to make pure, healthy, and tasty ghee that you and your family can trust every day.
We believe that good food starts with good intentions. That's why we use only natural ingredients, fresh cow's milk, and traditional methods to make our ghee — just like it was made in our homes for generations.

  • Pure - free from chemicals, preservatives, or anything artificial.
  • High Quality - rich in flavor, nutrients, and goodness.
  • Trusted - made with care, in clean environments, and tested for safety.
